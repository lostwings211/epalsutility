Metric: Total Community
Definition: All registered users with account status=active (has not been deactivated)

Metric: Total Community By Country
Definition: All registered users with account status=active, sort by Country (Top 25 and Others)

Metric: Actives
Definition: All registered users whose last login was on or after January 1st 2014

Metric: Actives By Country
Definition: All registered users whose last login was on or after January 1st 2014, sort by Country (Top 25 and Others)

Metric: New Users
Definition: All registered users with a joined date on or after January 1st, 2014
